How the Crop Selection System Works

The Crop Selection System operates through a combination of user input, sensor data, weather patterns, and historical yield information. Here's a simple overview:

  1. Input: The user enters soil type, location, weather, and other relevant conditions.
  2. Analysis: The system processes this data using AI models trained on agricultural datasets.
  3. Recommendation: It recommends the most suitable crops, including yield expectations and best planting times.

It is designed to support both individual farmers and agricultural extension agents.
