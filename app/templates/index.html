Welcome to the Crop Selection System

Empowering farmers and agricultural experts with data-driven crop selection and planting decisions based on soil, climate, and historical data.

Why Use This System?

The Crop Selection System is a smart decision-making tool designed to recommend the most suitable crops for your farmland. It uses AI, historical crop data, weather trends, and soil properties to guide farmers toward sustainable and profitable planting choices.

Features

  • 🌱 Soil and weather-based crop recommendations
  • 📊 Yield prediction and trend analysis
  • 📍 Regional adaptation support
  • 📷 Visual support and image upload (coming soon)
