About the Crop Selection System

The Crop Selection System is a decision-support web application designed to assist farmers, agronomists, and agricultural planners in selecting the most suitable crops based on local environmental conditions, soil properties, and seasonal patterns.

Developed as part of a sustainable agriculture initiative, the system integrates real-time data with historical records and agronomic knowledge to offer intelligent crop recommendations.

Our Mission

To empower smallholder and commercial farmers with AI-driven, location-specific crop advisory tools that enhance food security and promote sustainable land use.

Key Features

  • AI-based crop recommendation engine
  • Weather-aware seasonal planning
  • Soil and climate analysis integration
  • Historical yield performance guidance
  • User-friendly interface with mobile support

Team & Collaboration

The platform is maintained by a multidisciplinary team of engineers, data scientists, and agricultural specialists. We welcome partnerships with research institutions, governments, and NGOs focused on sustainable agriculture.
